Featured Games

Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a stunning, free-to-play open-world action RPG that thrusts players into a dynamic post-apocalyptic world with high-speed combat and a captivating story.

Genshin Impact

Explore the stunning, open world of Teyvat in Genshin Impact, a free-to-play action RPG. Master a unique elemental combat system, uncover a captivating story with a diverse cast of characters, and embark on an unforgettable adventure solo or with friends.

Honkai: Star Rail

Hop aboard the Astral Express in Honkai: Star Rail, a thrilling free-to-play space fantasy RPG. Explore diverse worlds, engage in strategic turn-based combat, and uncover a captivating story with memorable characters. Your journey across the stars awaits!

Honkai Impact 3rd

Take command of an elite squad of Valkyries in Honkai Impact 3rd, a stylish free-to-play action RPG. Unleash spectacular, fast-paced hack-and-slash combat and dive into a cinematic story as you fight to save humanity from the Honkai.

Azur Lane

Man a fleet of warships brought to life as beautiful girls in Azur Lane, a unique free-to-play side-scrolling shooter RPG. Collect and command a massive roster of characters, customize your fleet, and dive into an exciting battle system that combines strategy with explosive action.

NIKKE: Goddess of Victory

Lead the charge in a post-apocalyptic world with NIKKE: Goddess of Victory, a unique free-to-play sci-fi RPG shooter. Recruit and command a squad of powerful Nikkes in exhilarating third-person, cover-based combat. With a compelling story and an ever-expanding roster of characters, your mission to reclaim the surface and save humanity has never been more epic.

Blue Archive

Welcome to Kivotos, Sensei! In Blue Archive, a charming free-to-play anime RPG, you take on the role of an advisor to a squad of adorable and powerful students. Immerse yourself in a lighthearted, comedic story and engage in strategic, real-time combat to solve the city's mysteries and help your students with their daily adventures.

Umamusume: Pretty Derby

Gallop to glory in Umamusume: Pretty Derby, a unique free-to-play training RPG where you become a trainer for a team of beautiful "Horse Girls," each with their own personality and dreams. Guide your chosen Umamusume through rigorous training, compete in thrilling races, and watch as your bond with them grows. With an engaging story and strategic gameplay, you'll feel the rush of the track and the joy of victory!

Lost Sword

Embark on a mythical journey in Lost Sword, a charming free-to-play anime RPG. Dive into a rich, cinematic story and a world filled with lively and beautiful characters. Recruit your ideal team, unleash spectacular skills in vibrant 2D side-scrolling combat, and reclaim the Holy Sword in this epic isekai adventure!
